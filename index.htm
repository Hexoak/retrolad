Pixels

Pixel Art is a form of digital art that manipulates images at the pixel level.
We can create art in this form with a range of freely available software

The limitatations of older video games required this, but now, this asetetic is utilized in
everything from independent developers to AAA titles to invoke simplicity or nostalgia.

Canvas

Canvas is an element of HTML5 which allows scriptable rendering of images within modern browsers.
We can program canvas to create low level procedurely generated images and effects.

Utilizing layers within Canvas and our own art, we can add depth, lighting and other effects to enhance our artwork.
